Common Questions

Where is my game progress saved?

All progress — your ship, upgrades, gold, statistics, and achievements — is saved locally on your device. There is no account or sign-up. Because your save is stored on the device, clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app will remove your saved progress.

Does the game work offline?

Yes. Leave Her Johnny runs entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection to play.

Do you collect any of my data?

No. The game does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, and it has no ads or tracking. You can read the full Privacy Policy.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Please email us at the address above with as much detail as you can — what you were doing when the issue happened, and your device model and iOS version if you know them. It helps us track things down.
